Live Cheap. Live Well.

Modern life is expensive. We get that. With rising rents and stagnating wages, it's harder than ever to make a living. And when money isn't an issue, the demands of work can make it difficult to maintain an active social life. Why should we spend our lives performing an impossible balancing act?

That's why we created Talent Pool. Our app connects you to cities with the lowest rent, allowing you to compare your findings against the average nationwide salary. Just type in a zip code, and our easy-to-use interface yields the most up-to-date data for whichever size apartment you choose. Talent Pool assigns each query a rating of one to five based on which option saves you the most money, and stores each response so you can compare past and present searches. Registered users can make use of our messaging system to find locals in the area, connecting you with the people who will make your new city feel like home.

So what are you waiting for? Sign up for a free account and start living your best life!
